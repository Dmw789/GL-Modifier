INSTRUCTIONS

If translating to a lower grade level:
1. Maintain the same context.
2. Reduce the complexity of the sentence structure.
3. Ensure that the vocabularity is appropriate for the intended grade level.
4. Ensure that the concepts are appropriate for the intended grade level.
5. If the concepts introduced are too complex for the intended grade level, chnage the concepts to being more appropriate.

If translating to a higher grade level:
1. Maintain the same context.
2. Increase the complexity of the sentence structure.
3. Use more complex words when appropriate for the intended grade level.
4. Make the questions more challenging or require more creative thinking.
